About Us

Square Kulture WoodWorking Company was established in 2021 with the clear vision to hoist itself as a premium and sought-after furniture manufacturing firm.
Our key ojective is manufacturing hard wood furniture such as doors, kitchen cabinets and fittings, wardrobes. We also supply other types of furniture made from wrought-iron or steel.
At Square Kulture, we are technology driven, utiizing our team of design and craft specialists to churn out furniture that ultimately attracts respect and creates an elite environment
Our adherence to the use of high-grade wood, and best quality paints allow for longevity of our furniture.

Mr Augustus Adetona Badejo


MD/CEO

A seasoned and widely travelled furniture and wood work specialist. Detailed, meticulous and painstaking by nature, he has cut his teeth in the furniture world through the years, garnering technical and administrative know-how.
Mr Badejo worked with Mike Alibert Furniture Works Limited as General Manager from 2000 to 2014. He moved on to Agor homes, a furniture manufacturing firm in Lagos and worked from 2014 to 2021 as a furniture consultant and quality control development specialist. He is a team player.
Mr Badejo has lived in Ghana, West Africa, and in the United Kingdom, where he continued to build his career and technical skills. Married to a happy wife, he is a graduate of Political Science from Ogun state University, he loves and breed thorough-bred exotic dogs and sundry pets.

Torezi Anthony Obaro (ANIPR)


Sales/Marketing Director

Mr. Obaro is the marketer's marketer. He brings his marketing skills and experience to the management team as Marketing/Sales Director.
A graduate of Mass Communications from the Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma Edo State, he has heightened and cross-category experience in marketing, public relations and brand management. He has handled FMCG and high-end luxury brands.
He loves to engage in charity work, horticulture and copy writing as hobbies. Mr. Obaro is happily married.
