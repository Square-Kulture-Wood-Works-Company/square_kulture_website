About us

Square Kulture Wood Working Company was established in 2021 with the clear vision to hoist itself as a premium and sought-after furniture manufacturing firm.
Our key objective is manufacturing hard wood furniture such as doors, kitchen cabinets and fittings, wardrobes. We also supply other types of furniture made from wrought- iron or steel. At Square Kulture we are technology-driven, utilizing our team of design and craft specialists to churn out furniture that ultimately attracts respect and creates an elite environment. Our adherence to the use of high-grade wood, and best quality paints allow for longevity of our furniture. Our services include the following:
* Door Production and installation
* Fitted Kitchen Cabinets
* Wardrobes
* Office and home furniture
* Wall Cladding
* POP and general interior décor work

Our Leaders

CEO/MD

Mr Justus Augustus Badejo

Mr Justus Augustus Badejo is a seasoned and widely travelled furniture and wood work specialist. Detailed, meticulous and painstaking by nature, he has cut his teeth in the furniture world through the years, garnering technical and administrative know-how.

Mr Badejo worked wit Mike Alibert Funiture Works Limited as General Manager from 2000 to 2014. He later joined Agor Homes, a furniture manufacturing firm in Lagos from 2014 to 2021 as a furniture consultant and quality control development specialist. He is a Team player.

Mr Badejo has lived in Ghana, West Africa, and the United Kingdom, where he further honed his technical skills.

A graduate of Political Science from Ogun State University, Mr Badejo is a lover and breeder of exotic dogs and sundry pets as a hobby. He is happily married
