About us

Square Kulture Wood Working Company was established in 2021 with the clear vision to hoist itself as a premium and sought-after furniture manufacturing firm. Our key objective is manufacturing hard wood furniture such as doors, kitchen cabinets and fittings, wardrobes. We also supply other types of furniture made from wrought- iron or steel. At Square Kulture we are technology-driven, utilizing our team of design and craft specialists to churn out furniture that ultimately attracts respect and creates an elite environment. Our adherence to the use of high-grade wood, and best quality paints allow for longevity of our furniture. Our services include the following: • Door Production and installation • Fitted Kitchen Cabinets • Wardrobes • Office and home furniture • Wall Cladding • POP and general interior décor work
